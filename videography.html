Film without the performance

Natural storytelling, without turning your day into a film set

I began with wedding photography and later brought videography into Nimble so couples could relive more than a single frame — the sound of the vows, the laughter during the speeches and the energy of the dance floor.

The approach stays exactly the same: calm, unobtrusive and low-pressure. I will guide you when it helps, blend into the background when it does not, and let the day unfold naturally.

You do not need to act, pose or perform. Enjoy your wedding — I’ll take care of remembering how it felt.
